Movies that pass the test:

The Princess Diaries

2001

Director: Garry Marshall

Synopsis:
Shy teenager Mia Thermopolis (Anne Hathaway) is thrown for a loop when, out of the blue, she learns the astonishing news that she's a real-life princess.

Easy A

2010

Director: Will Gluck

Synopsis:
A clean-cut high school student relies on the school's rumor mill to advance her social and financial standing.

Soul Surfer

2011

Director: Sean McNamara

Synopsis:
Surfer Bethany Hamilton overcomes the odds and her fears of returning to the water after losing her arm to a shark.

Bird Box

2018

Director: Susanne Bier

Synopsis:
Five years after an ominous unseen presence drives most of society to suicide, a mother and her children try to reach safety.

A Star is Born

2018

Directors: Bradley Cooper

Synopsis:
A musician helps a young singer find fame as age and alcoholism send his own career into a downward spiral.

Captain Marvel

2019

Directors: Anna Boden, Ryan Fleck

Synopsis:
Carol Danvers becomes one of the universe's most powerful heroes when Earth is caught in a galactic war between two alien races.

The Devil Wears Prada

2006

Director: David Frankel

Synopsis:
A smart but sensible new graduate lands a job as an assistant to Miranda Priestly, the demanding editor-in-chief of a high fashion magazine.

Black Panther

2018

Directors: Ryan Coogler

Synopsis:
T'Challa, heir to the hidden but advanced kingdom of Wakanda, must step forward to lead his people into a new future and must confront a challenger from his country's past.
